ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LIONS

Lions are known as the “king of the jungle.” They are social animals that live in prides and are known for their majestic manes.

ELEPHANT

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and long trunks.

GEMSBOK

The Gemsbok is a large antelope known for its striking appearance and long, straight horns. It is well adapted to arid environments.

GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs. They primarily feed on leaves from tall trees.

GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest primates and are known for their strength and intelligence. They live in family groups and are primarily herbivorous.

MONKEY

Monkeys are highly social animals known for their agility and intelligence. They live in troops and have diverse diets.

PANDA

Pandas are known for their distinctive black and white fur. They primarily eat bamboo and are native to China.

WARTHOG

Warthogs are wild pigs known for their tusks and distinctive facial warts. They are found in sub-Saharan Africa and are well adapted to savanna habitats.

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia, known for their tree-dwelling lifestyle and diet primarily consisting of eucalyptus leaves.
