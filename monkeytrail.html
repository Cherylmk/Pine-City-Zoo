MONKEY TRAIL

Walk through the Monkey Trail and experience the playful antics of various monkey species in a naturalistic setting. Watch them swing, climb, and interact with each other in their specially designed habitat.

Featured Species:

  • Capuchin Monkeys
  • Squirrel Monkeys
  • Spider Monkeys
